I was born and raised in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. Naturally, I love the beach, and I first discovered my love for photography while taking photos of the beautiful Florida coastline. As a teenager I attended Dreyfoos School of the Arts, where I studied digital media. It was at Dreyfoos that I began to explore artistic portraiture. It was also in high school that I began working as a wedding photographer, and I was instantly hooked. Beyond just taking photos, I loved the opportunity to get know so many different people and help capture their dream day. Since starting my journey as a wedding photographer, my work has been featured in a number of magazines including The Knot. I have also held numerous workshops for budding photographers and been been a guest teacher in workshops around the country. I feel so blessed to be doing what I love every day!
